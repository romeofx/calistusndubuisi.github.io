UPCOMING PROJECT

ABOUT IMMER -"AN INDEPENDENT PLATFORM FOR INDEPENDENT ENTERTAINERS"

• Empowers entertainers to coordinate live events and shows directly with venues.

• Manage everything from dates, venues, marketing, and ticket sales within one unified platform.

• Attendees can access a broad variety of live events through ticket purchases, local event passes, and entertainer subscriptions.

• Democratizes ticket pricing, putting it in the hands of entertainers to make events more affordable for fans.

PROBLEMS - "EVENT PRICING IS OUT OF CONTROL!"

• Exorbitant ticket prices: Live entertainment hindered by a 50% increase in concert ticket prices over the past decade.

• Promoter domination: Promoters control entertainers and venues, taking a significant portion of ticket sales.

• Limited opportunities for independent talent: Struggle to secure bookings due to promoter control and domination.

• Affordability challenge: Post-pandemic, live event pricing has risen beyond $200, $500, and even above $1,000 for single admissions

VALUE PROPOSITION - THE VALUE OF IMMER:

• Control: immer gives entertainers and hosts more control over their events. They can set their own ticket prices, keep more of the revenue, and have more control over the production of their events.

• Affordability: Immer is more affordable for both entertainers and fans. Entertainers can save money on fees, and fans can find tickets at a lower price.

• Convenience: Immer is more convenient for both entertainers and fans.

• Discovery: Immer makes it easier for fans to discover new events. The platform features a user-friendly interface that makes it easy to search for events by location, genre, and date. Fans can also create custom event lists and receive notifications about upcoming events.
